### Initial Consultation

A one-to-one discussion with a Neurodivergent Specialist to explore your difficulties and agree on collaborative support options tailored to your needs.

</div>
<div class="card">

### Differential Screenings

Clinician-conducted screening using NICE-recommended, research-validated tools to identify potential neurodivergence. Includes a formal referral letter for GP submission, which may help reduce NHS waiting times.

</div>
<div class="card">

### Neurodivergent Counselling

Forty-five minute sessions available via video, phone, messenger, or email. Led by trained, neurodiverse counsellors experienced with neurodivergent clients.

</div>
<div class="card">

### Neurodivergent Coaching

Forty-five minute one-to-one sessions with a trained coach who has experience coaching neurodiverse individuals and is themselves neurodiverse. Sessions support post-diagnosis life reframing and navigating neurodivergence in neurotypical environments.

</div>
<div class="card">

### Training & Consultancy

Professional development for businesses, educational institutions, and charities. Covers staff training, environmental consultations, accessibility audits, and website reviews.

</div>
</div>

## Autism Assessment

We support neurodivergent wellbeing through collaborative, affirmative approaches rather than deficit-focused models. Our assessments use gold-standard tools (ADOS-2, ADI-R) with multidisciplinary team review.
