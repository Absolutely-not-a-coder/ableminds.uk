We are committed to helping neurodivergent individuals with all aspects of their lives through our client-focussed services.

## Who We Work With

We welcome outreach from:

- County Commissioning Groups (CCGs)
- Local Authorities
- Educational establishments
- Charities and community interest groups
- Other agencies and establishments identifying a need for specialist services

## Referrals

We accept referrals and will work alongside your organisation to reduce assessment waiting times.

To discuss a partnership, contact us at [ableminds@mailfence.com](mailto:ableminds@mailfence.com).
